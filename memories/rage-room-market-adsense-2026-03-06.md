# Memory: Rage Room SEO Demand + AdSense Monetization Baseline

Date: 2026-03-06
Owner: vijaychauhan
Topic: rage rooms (US-focused)

## Demand Snapshot
- Global demand reference used: ~184,000 monthly searches for rage-room terms (industry article citing Ahrefs).
- Working inference for US share: ~35% to 50%.
- Estimated US demand range from that inference: ~64,000 to ~92,000 monthly searches.
- Market footprint reference: one directory reports 305 rage rooms across 246 US cities.

## Monetization Baseline (AdSense)
- Core formula used: Estimated earnings = (Pageviews / 1000) x Page RPM.
- Planning Page RPM range for this niche/local-intent content: $3 to $8 (assumption band, not guarantee).

## Scenario Estimates
- 10,000 pageviews/month => $30 to $80/month
- 50,000 pageviews/month => $150 to $400/month
- 100,000 pageviews/month => $300 to $800/month
- 250,000 pageviews/month => $750 to $2,000/month

## Target Math for $1,000/month
- At $4 RPM => ~250,000 pageviews/month
- At $6 RPM => ~166,667 pageviews/month
- At $8 RPM => ~125,000 pageviews/month

## Notes
- AdSense alone is likely low-to-moderate revenue unless traffic is very large.
- Prefer adding non-AdSense monetization later (lead gen/affiliate/direct deals) for better unit economics.
