## Core Concept
Ahrefs studied 75,000 brands and found that AI visibility correlates most strongly with off-site brand presence, especially YouTube mentions, web mentions, branded anchors, and branded search demand.

## Key Takeaways
- AI visibility is not mainly a website-only problem. Off-site presence is one of the strongest predictors of whether a brand appears in AI answers.
- YouTube mentions showed the strongest correlation with AI visibility, stronger than many classic SEO metrics.
- Branded web mentions and branded anchors are major signals, reinforcing the idea that entity presence across the web matters more than isolated page optimization.
- Branded search volume correlates strongly enough to treat demand generation as part of AI search strategy.
- AI brand visibility appears to be a compound outcome of awareness, distribution, search presence, and entity consistency.
- This is one of the most strategically useful Ahrefs studies for agent orchestration because it directly changes channel prioritization.

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
- **75k brand correlation study**: identify which observable factors move with AI visibility
- **Off-site entity model**: mentions, anchors, search demand, and platform presence reinforce brand inclusion
- **Demand generation loop**: brand search and mentions likely support one another

## Data/Stats
- Published: December 12, 2025
- Sample size: 75,000 brands
- Ahrefs highlights YouTube mentions as the strongest observed correlation at roughly `0.737`
- Strong additional correlations include branded web mentions, branded anchors, and branded search volume

## How The Team Should Use This
- **Pinchy (Orchestrator)**: Shift some effort from pure on-site publishing to brand-distribution systems that increase mentions and demand.
- **Coral (SEO)**: Stop treating brand queries as vanity metrics; they are visibility infrastructure.
- **Charles**: Prioritize YouTube and external mention generation because they are directly relevant to AI visibility.
- **Current (Marketing)**: Treat PR, partnerships, and cross-platform brand mentions as search investments, not just awareness plays.
- **Key insight for @learnaiwithvijay**: If the brand is not being talked about across the web, AI systems have less reason to surface it.
