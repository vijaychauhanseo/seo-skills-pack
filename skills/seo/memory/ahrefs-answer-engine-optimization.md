## Core Concept
Ahrefs’ AEO framework is simple: make your content easier for AI systems to retrieve, understand, and cite by closing answer gaps and rewriting pages into a more direct, machine-friendly format.

## Key Takeaways
- AI search optimization is often an editing and structure problem, not only a “rank better” problem.
- If the cited source is already close to the correct answer, adding a concise FAQ or declarative answer block can improve citation quality.
- If your site does not cover the answer clearly, publish a new section or new page to close the gap.
- Answer formatting matters: lead with direct statements, clear subjects, and concise answers before stories or elaboration.
- Third-party sources also matter. If AI systems keep using external sources for your brand information, update those surfaces too.
- The strongest use of this article is as a content-rewrite playbook for pages that rank or get cited but do not control the narrative well enough.

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
- **Gap-closing workflow**: decide whether to add a section to an existing page or create a new page
- **Answer-first formatting**: headings + direct answers + clean declarative sentences
- **Third-party correction loop**: fix misinformation where AI is sourcing it, not just on your own site
