## Core Concept
This Ahrefs update matters less as product news and more as workflow infrastructure: it shows how Brand Radar and related endpoints are becoming programmable systems that agents can plug into reporting, AI visibility tracking, and competitive monitoring.

## Key Takeaways
- Brand Radar now exposes cited pages and cited domains through API endpoints, making AI citation monitoring automatable.
- Ahrefs also added AI Share of Voice distribution and historical endpoints, which means visibility can be tracked over time rather than inspected manually.
- This is important for agent design because AI visibility reporting can now be built into recurring workflows, not one-off screenshots.
- The product direction is clear: Ahrefs is building a visibility layer across search, AI, video, and brand mentions, not just backlinks and rankings.
- Strong agents should treat these endpoints as operational intelligence, not “nice to have” reporting extras.
- This update is a signal that SEO tooling is shifting toward entity and AI visibility monitoring at scale.

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
- **Programmable Brand Radar**: cited sources + AI Share of Voice + history endpoints
- **Automation-first reporting**: monitor AI citations and trend shifts without manual exports
- **AI visibility ops**: connect citation data to content planning and competitor tracking
