## Core Concept
Topicality (T*) is Google's quantitative relevance score that measures how closely a document and site align with a given query's topic cluster — it's Chapter 2 of the leaked Strategic SEO 2025 ebook, revealing that "relevance" is now algorithmically scored, not just inferred.

## Key Takeaways
- T* (Topicality star score) is a real leaked signal in Google's Content Warehouse — relevance is quantified
- It's not just about keyword matching — T* measures topical alignment across the entire document and site
- The ABC framework for relevance: Aboutness (what the page covers), Breadth (how completely it covers the topic), Coherence (how well it fits the site's topical identity)
- Sites that are topically coherent benefit from a site-wide relevance boost — not just individual pages
- This is a preview/ebook chapter format — deeper analysis requires the full Strategic SEO 2025 ebook
- Topicality score interacts with Quality score (Q*) — high-relevance + high-quality = maximum ranking potential
- The implication: thin, broad content gets a low T* score; deep, focused content gets a high T* score

## Frameworks/Methods Covered
- **T* Score**: The topicality metric from Google's Content Warehouse leak
- **ABC Framework**: Aboutness + Breadth + Coherence as the three dimensions of topicality
- **Site-wide vs. Page-level Topicality**: How individual page T* scores aggregate to affect the whole site
- **T* x Q* Interaction**: The combined effect of topicality and quality scores on final ranking position
